# Audio Instructions

Place your audio instruction files in this directory:

- **english.mp3** - English language instruction audio
- **telugu.mp3** - Telugu language instruction audio

## Format Requirements

- Format: MP3
- Recommended bitrate: 128 kbps or higher
- The audio will play before speech recognition starts for each statement

## Example Content

The audio should contain instructions like:

- "Please read the following statement clearly into your microphone"
- Or in Telugu: "దయచేసి కింది వాక్యాన్ని మీ మైక్రోఫోన్‌లో స్పష్టంగా చదవండి"
